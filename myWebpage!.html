My Favorite Anime Quotes

As I always say, anime is worthwhile to watch because of great storytelling, comedy, and the life lessons anime can teach you. Anime teaches you by conveying their message through the character's dialogue, making anime quotes popular amongst many anime fans. Below, I will share my top 3 favorite anime quotes by different anime characters.

For more info on these characters and quotes go to Wiki Fandom.





3. Natsu Dragneel

Quote:
"You don't die for your friends, you live for them."-Natsu Dragneel

This quote from Natsu shows his love for his friends. He tells the viewers in this quote that your friends would want you to live for them rather than die.


2. Izuku Midoriya

Quote:
"Sometimes I feel like I'm a failure. Like there's no hope for me, But even so, I'm not going to give up. Ever!"-Izuku Midoriya

Izuku's quote encourages viewers to never give up and perservere. This quote is powerful because it shoowcases his strong will to keep pushing.


1. Tetsuya Kuroko

Quote:
"So as long as I don't give up, the possibility of winning will never fall to zero."-Tetsuya Kuroko

This quote also shows a theme of perserverance and determination. Tetsuya believes that not giving up gives him a chance to still succeed.
